1 Not Financial Advice

Plenee is not a registered investment adviser, broker-dealer, financial planner, or financial counselor under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 or any applicable state law. Plenee has not registered with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) or any state securities regulator as an investment adviser.

Rule: Nothing in Plenee — including fee analysis, debt payoff scenarios, idle cash calculations, refinancing breakdowns, and cash flow projections — constitutes personalized investment advice, financial planning advice, tax advice, or legal advice. These are analytical tools and scenario models, not recommendations.

Plenee's outputs are informational in nature and are intended to help you understand your current financial picture. They do not account for all factors relevant to your specific situation, risk tolerance, goals, or overall financial health. Before making any financial decisions based on information from Plenee, you should consult with a licensed and qualified financial professional.

Plenee does not guarantee any financial outcome, including but not limited to savings, interest reduction, or debt elimination. All projections are estimates based on inputs you provide and may not reflect actual future results.

This disclosure extends fully to Plenee Academy, the platform's library of educational articles and reference content. Academy content is financial information and general education only, written for a general audience — it does not constitute personalized financial advice and does not account for your individual accounts, circumstances, or goals.

3 No Fiduciary Relationship

Use of Plenee does not create a fiduciary relationship between you and Plenee Co. The term "fiduciary" has a specific legal meaning that applies to certain licensed professionals (registered investment advisers, attorneys, trustees). Plenee is not a registered fiduciary in any legal capacity.

Where Plenee describes its business model as acting "only for you" or "without conflicts of interest" (no advertising, no referral fees, no data sales), this describes our commercial incentive structure — not a legally defined fiduciary duty. We believe these commitments align our interests with yours, but they do not constitute legal fiduciary obligations.

Plenee's commitment to users:

  • We receive no commissions, referral fees, or compensation from any financial institution, lender, or product provider we mention or analyze.
  • We do not sell or share your financial data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • Our revenue comes from user subscriptions, not from third parties with interests in your financial decisions.

These commitments are commercial and ethical — not legally enforceable fiduciary duties. You should treat Plenee as a transparent analytical tool, not as a licensed professional acting on your behalf.

4 Data Accuracy & Sources

Plenee relies on data imported from your linked financial accounts (via Plaid), data you enter manually, and reference data maintained by Plenee (such as benchmark interest rates and HYSA rates). Plenee does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of any data.

Factors that may affect data accuracy include:

  • Delays in Plaid transaction synchronization (typically 1–3 business days)
  • Rounding and estimation in fee/interest classification algorithms
  • Reference rates (HYSA benchmarks, market rates) updated monthly — may not reflect current conditions
  • Manual account entries that are not verified against source institutions
  • Transaction categorization that may be misclassified by automated pattern matching

All dollar figures in Plenee — fees identified, interest calculated, savings projected — are estimates. They are based on available transaction data and should be verified against your actual account statements before being relied upon for any financial decision.

Statistics and benchmarks cited by Plenee (e.g., average overdraft fees, national average APRs, typical savings account rates) are sourced from publicly available industry data and are provided for context and illustration. They may not reflect your specific institution's current rates or your individual circumstances.

Where Plenee displays projected figures (e.g., estimated annual savings, debt-free date), these projections assume consistent income, spending patterns, and rates based on recent historical data. Actual results will vary.

5 Third-Party Services & Plaid

Plenee uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. to connect to and retrieve data from your financial institutions. When you link an account through Plenee, you are also subject to Plaid's Privacy Policy and End User Privacy Policy.

  • Plenee accesses your financial data in read-only mode via Plaid. Plenee cannot initiate transactions, move money, or modify account settings.
  • Plaid may store credentials and access tokens separately from Plenee. Plenee does not store your bank login credentials.
  • Data retrieved via Plaid is subject to the terms of your financial institution's account agreement.

Plenee may also use the following third-party data services for market reference data:

  • CoinGecko API — cryptocurrency spot prices (if crypto holdings are tracked)
  • Coinbase API — gold and silver spot prices (if precious metals are tracked)
  • Zillow — estimated property values for real estate linked in the net worth tracker (Zestimate is a third-party estimate, not an appraisal)

Plenee is not responsible for the accuracy, availability, or content of third-party data sources. Reference to these services does not constitute an endorsement by Plenee.

6 Privacy & Financial Data Protection (GLBA)

Plenee collects, maintains, and protects your nonpublic personal financial information (NPI) in accordance with the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA) and the FTC's Safeguards Rule (16 C.F.R. Part 314).

What we collect: Account balances, transaction history, loan terms, bill due dates, and other financial data retrieved via Plaid or entered by you.

How we use it: Solely to provide Plenee's analysis features to you. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

Who we share it with:

  • Plaid — for account data retrieval (subject to Plaid's privacy policy)
  • Supabase — our database and authentication infrastructure provider, under data processing agreement
  • Service providers acting strictly on our behalf under confidentiality obligations
  • We do not share your NPI with financial institutions, advertisers, or data brokers

Security: Plenee implements a written information security program with encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and incident response procedures as required by the FTC Safeguards Rule.

Opt-out: You may request that we limit sharing of your NPI beyond what is required to provide the service at any time by contacting us at privacy@plenee.com.

Your data access rights (CFPB Section 1033): Under Dodd-Frank §1033, you have the right to view and revoke Plenee's access to each connected financial institution at any time. You can manage connected accounts and revoke access per institution from the Connected Accounts screen in app settings. Upon revocation, Plenee will immediately stop syncing that account and schedule deletion of all associated data within 30 days. You may also request immediate deletion of all your data at any time.

A full Privacy Policy governing all data collection and use is available at plenee.com/privacy.

7 California & State Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the CPRA:

  • Right to Know — You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete — You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct — You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out — You may opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. Plenee does not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information — Financial data is considered sensitive personal information under CPRA. We use it only to provide our services.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at privacy@plenee.com or via the data request form in the Plenee app settings.

California Financial Information Privacy Act (CalFIPA): California residents have additional rights regarding the sharing of financial information with affiliated and non-affiliated companies. Plenee does not share your financial information with affiliates or non-affiliates for marketing purposes without your affirmative consent (opt-in).

Residents of other states with enacted privacy laws (Virginia VCDPA, Colorado CPA, Connecticut CTDPA, and others) have similar rights. Contact us at the address below to exercise your rights under applicable state law.

  • Act as a payment processor, money transmitter, or custodian

If Plenee ever introduces a money movement feature in a future version, it will be powered by a licensed payment processor and disclosed separately, with applicable terms and disclosures presented prior to use.

13 European Users — GDPR / UK GDPR

Plenee is currently available in the United States only. We do not accept sign-ups from users in the European Union or United Kingdom at this time.

When Plenee expands to the EU or UK, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR will govern all processing of personal data for users in those regions. The following rights and obligations will apply:

Lawful basis for processing: Plenee will process your financial data on the basis of contractual necessity — the data is required to deliver the service you signed up for. Certain secondary uses (e.g., product analytics) will be based on legitimate interest or explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

Your rights under GDPR:

  • Right of access — Request a copy of all personal data Plenee holds about you
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data; honored within 30 days
  • Right to rectification — Correct inaccurate manually-entered data
  • Right to data portability — Export your data in machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to restrict processing — Request that we limit how we use your data while a dispute is resolved

International data transfers: Plenee's infrastructure (Supabase) and data aggregation provider (Plaid) are based in the United States. EU/UK user data transferred to the US will be protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent approved mechanisms under GDPR. Data Processing Agreements will be in place with all sub-processors before EU sign-ups are accepted.

Data retention: Raw transaction data is retained for up to 24 months from the date of import (matching the Plaid maximum history window). Account metadata is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days following account closure. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

For GDPR inquiries, contact us at privacy@plenee.com. EU/UK users may also lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority.
